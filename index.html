Origin

Our journal was founded in 2001 with the mission to promote academic research and scholarly articles in various disciplines. It has since grown into an internationally recognized platform that attracts scholars from around the world.

Since its inception, the journal has been dedicated to providing high-quality, peer-reviewed articles that contribute to the advancement of knowledge in various fields of study.

Editorial Board

The editorial board consists of esteemed professionals and experts who oversee the peer review process and ensure the highest quality of published work.

Our editorial board includes distinguished scholars, professors, and researchers from top universities globally, each bringing a wealth of knowledge and expertise to the journal.

Submission Guidelines

Authors wishing to submit to our journal should follow these guidelines:

  • All manuscripts must be in English and typed in Arial, 12-point font.
  • The manuscript should be no more than 7,500 words, including references and appendices.
  • References must follow APA citation style.
